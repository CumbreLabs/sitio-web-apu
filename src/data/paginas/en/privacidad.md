## Privacy Policy

APU Mountain Travel respects your privacy. This page describes how we handle the
information you share with us.

### Information we collect

We only collect information you voluntarily send us when you contact us via WhatsApp,
email or phone to book an experience or ask a question.

### Use of information

We use your data solely to respond to your request, coordinate trips and give you
information about our services. We do not sell or share your information with third
parties.

### Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand, in aggregate and anonymously, how the
page is browsed. You can disable cookies from your browser.
